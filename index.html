Tasty Tuna Salad!

Recipe by: Chanler Fox

Ingredients you'll need:

  • Relish
  • Canned Tuna
  • Mayonnaise
  • Your choice of bread

Optional Ingredients:

  • Red Onion
  • Cheddar Cheese

Necessary Supplies:

  • Bowl
  • Fork
  • Plate

Putting it all togther:

  1. Add canned tuna to bowl
  2. Add 3 TBSP of mayonnaise to bowl
  3. Add 2 TBSP of relish to bowl
  4. Use fork to combine ingredients
  5. Set bread on plate and apply your tuna salad to the bread
  6. Put pieces of bread together and enjoy!

Optional:

  • Add cheddar cheese slice
  • Toast on pan
  • Now you have a tuna melt!
